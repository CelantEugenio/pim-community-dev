# Typography

In Akeneo products, we use the [Lato open source typeface](https://www.latofonts.com/)

## Usage

Typography guides the users to read and understand the hierarchy of information. Good typographic usage and proper use of character styles make it possible to manage the display of the content, keeping it useful, simple, and effective.

### Scale

The font scale enables you to indicate content hierarchy. These sizes and styles are designed to balance content with better readability.

### Font weight

The weight of the font is an essential typographic variable that can increase and differentiate the hierarchy of content. Combinations of weight and font size must be carefully balanced. A bold weight will always be more important than a lighter font of the same size. However, a lighter font may have a higher hierarchical rank than a bold font if its size is much bigger than the one in bold.

### Type color

The color of the characters must be carefully considered. Readability and accessibility must be at the top of your mind. Keep the neutral type color in the body text.

## Title

Use the title style to summarize the content that follows the title. Careful linking of titles makes the hierarchy of content clear and improves readability. There can only be one title-regular on the same page.
